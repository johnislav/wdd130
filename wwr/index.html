Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote an exciting, affordable, and safe white water rafting experience for individuals and families. Our goal is to help people build lasting memories in nature.

Audience

Our target audience includes outdoor enthusiasts, families, and working adults looking to escape daily stress. The website works great on mobile and desktop, letting users learn about trip options and book online.
